老友粉

OLD FRIEND NOODLE

Brief Introduction

Old friend noodle is the most important traditional food in Nanning City.

In 2007 it was selected Nanning announced the 26 non-material cultural heritage list

together with Liuzhou snail noodle, Guilin rice noodles, Wuzhou fried river noodle

consist of Guangxi "four rice noodles"

The taset of Old Friend Noodle is very spicy and sour.

Nanning people think that eating hot and sour old friends noodle in the summer can be appetizing

Eating it in the winter can keep warm

Old Friend Noodle is a very popular food amount all the classes


Ingredients

  • rice Noodles
  • meat
  • chopped green onion
  • pepper
  • salt black bean
  • shredded ginger
